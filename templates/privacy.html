1. What We Collect

We collect only the information necessary to provide our expense tracking services. This includes your account details such as name and email address, along with the expense entries and categories you choose to record.

We do not collect sensitive financial information such as bank account credentials or credit card numbers.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to operate and improve the Spendly application. We use it to display your expense summaries, generate reports, and personalize your experience.

We do not sell, share, or disclose your personal information to third parties under any circumstances.

3. Data Storage

All your data is stored securely in a SQLite database. We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your data against unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction.

While we strive to protect your data, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

4. Your Rights

You have full control over your data. Here's what you can do at any time:

  • Access all your stored expense data and personal information
  • Edit or update your account details and expense records
  • Delete any individual expense entries or your entire account
  • Request a copy of all data we hold about you

5. Cookies

Spendly uses session cookies to keep you logged in and remember your preferences. These cookies are essential for the application to function correctly.

We do not use tracking cookies, analytics cookies, or any third-party advertising cookies. Your browsing privacy is respected.

6. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please reach out to us through the application's contact channels.

We review and may update this policy periodically. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision.
